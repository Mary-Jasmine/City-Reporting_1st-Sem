What We Do

Our project, Municipality Incident Reporting, is an online platform that allows barangays to report incidents such as accidents, emergencies, or safety issues. The system serves as a central repository where residents can submit reports with relevant details, photos, or videos. It provides municipalities with an organized way to track and review reported incidents over time.

Purpose of the System

The system aims to improve the documentation and transparency of incidents within the community. While it does not guarantee immediate action by local officials, it ensures that incidents are officially recorded and can be reviewed by the municipal authorities for proper follow-up.

Our Mission

To provide a reliable platform that enables barangays to report incidents accurately and efficiently.

Our Vision

To create a structured and accessible system that helps municipalities maintain records of incidents and monitor community safety over time.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to creating a system that strengthens documentation and communication between citizens and municipalities. Our team worked collaboratively to ensure the platform is user-friendly, reliable, and organized. We believe in transparency, efficiency, and community empowerment through technology.
